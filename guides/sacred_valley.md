# Sacred Valley Quick Guide

## Why two days?
The Sacred Valley is not only a road to Machu Picchu. Travelling slowly allows acclimatization before hiking Waynapicchu.

### Stops
- Pisac – ruins and market
- Chinchero – weaving traditions
- Moray – Inca agricultural terraces
- Maras – salt pans
- Ollantaytambo – fortress and train departure

### Photography
- Morning: Pisac
- Afternoon: Maras
- Sunset: Ollantaytambo

### Tips
- Carry cash.
- Bring water and sunscreen.
- Roads are winding.
